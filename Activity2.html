K.G.F

"K.G.F" (Kolar Gold Fields) is an Indian period action film series, specifically a Kannada-language series, created by Prashanth Neel. It's set in the 1970s and 80s, focusing on the life of Rocky, a young man from Mumbai who rises to power within the criminal underworld and eventually becomes a key figure in the Kolar Gold Fields (KGF).
